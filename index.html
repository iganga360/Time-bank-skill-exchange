How Time Banking Works

1. Give Time

Offer a skill—whether it’s tutoring, gardening, or pet-sitting—to a neighbor.

2. Earn Credits

For every hour you give, you earn one time-credit in your account.

3. Spend Credits

Use your earned credits to receive help from any other member in the network.
